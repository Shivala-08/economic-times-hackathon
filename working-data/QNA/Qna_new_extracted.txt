Tip for your evaluation dataset
For each benchmark Q&A, store:
● Question
● Gold answer
● Source document (e.g., OISD-105, OISD-192, OSHWC Code, Company HSE Manual)
● Section/Clause/Page
● Expected citations
● Difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard)
● Topic tag (PTW, LOTO, Fire Safety, PPE, Confined Space, etc.)
This structure makes the dataset much more useful for objectively evaluating retrieval accuracy,
answer correctness, and citation quality.
